Description

Join us for our open house on Sunday, June 23rd at 1:00 to 3:00. You will appreciate the house even more after you tour.

Offered at $2,995.00 a month, AMP Property Management is pleased to present a unique opportunity to lease this historic El Granada home. Originally constructed in 1905, the house is spacious with 3 bedrooms, and 2 full baths. Rumor has it that the street was actually named after the 100 ft. Palm in the front yard. Perfect for the family who loves coastal living, there is excellent sunshine here in the "sunbelt", and El Granada easily has the best weather in the entire Half Moon Bay area. Expertly maintained, and lovingly restored and renovated inside and out, the house is as charming, as it is elegant.

One of the very first homes built in El Granada (see picture), this amazing piece of early 1900's architecture features a wonderful floor plan for daily living. There is the original decorative wood burning fireplace, and bay windows in the living room. With approximately 1,800 square feet of living space, you will marvel at the high ceilings with encased beams, dramatic panoramic tree filled windows, beautiful chocolate brown hardwood floors, built in floor to ceiling book shelves (with ladder), and crown molding throughout the first level.

The dramatic staircase leading up to the amazing second story features beautiful hardwood banisters, handrails, and wrought iron balusters. The upstairs has amazing natural light through the strategic use of seven skylights, and a filtered ocean view from the double window at the front of the house. There is a completely updated full bathroom, and two large closets as well as a generous additional storage area. This upstairs has vaulted ceilings, wall to wall carpet with a 10 lb pad and beautiful wood treads on the stairs to the upper level. The upstairs will make a serene home office, or a child's bedroom they will never forget.

The updated Kitchen has a lovely pass through to the dining room, and has beautiful newer Corian counters, a new tile floor, a brand new dishwasher, newer stove and refrigerator. Their is a large redwood deck which wraps around the side of the house. The master bedroom is very large with a large window at the back and a new double-pane slider to access the deck. There is an attached tiled shower room adjacent to the master bathroom, featuring unique double shower faucets, and a "retro" sunken tub.

There is an inside laundry with top loading washer and front loading dryer. There is a sizable yard on the front, side and back, with ample room for a large organic vegetable garden. The house is close to the Harbor, walking distance to the beaches, restaurants, shopping and more.

Touch up on the interior paint is being done today (6/21), the outside of the house will be professionally repainted this Saturday, 6/22. More pictures to follow with update on Sunday.

In addition to some of the best surfing in the world, El Granada offers seaside living at it's best. El Granada serves as a coastal retreat for anyone looking for respite from the high-tech lifestyle and on-the-go world of Silicon Valley. Quaint bed & breakfasts, beaches, outdoor activities, wellness centers and delicious restaurants make El Granada ideal for lives centered around peaceful living.
